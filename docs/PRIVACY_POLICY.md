# VyapaarBandhu — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Who We Are

VyapaarBandhu is a GST document management and draft preparation SaaS tool operated for
Indian small businesses and their Chartered Accountants (CAs). We are NOT a financial
services provider. We do not file GST returns. We prepare filing-ready drafts that CAs
review, approve, and upload to the GSTN portal.

## 2. Data We Collect

| Data | Why | How Long |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp phone number | Identify you, send messages | 7 years after last activity |
| Business name and owner name | Display in CA dashboard | 7 years |
| GSTIN (15-character tax ID) | Tax calculations, state detection | 7 years |
| Invoice photos | OCR extraction of financial data | 3 years |
| Invoice amounts (taxable, CGST, SGST, IGST, total) | ITC draft calculation | 7 years |
| Invoice seller details (name, GSTIN) | Compliance checks | 7 years |
| CA account details (email, ICAI membership) | Authentication | Account lifetime + 1 year |

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- Extract invoice details from photos you send via WhatsApp
- Calculate draft Input Tax Credit (ITC) for your CA to review
- Send you filing deadline reminders
- Generate GSTR-3B draft JSON and filing summary PDFs for your CA

We do NOT use your data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or credit scoring.

## 4. Who Sees Your Data

- **Your CA:** The Chartered Accountant who onboarded you sees all your invoice data
- **VyapaarBandhu team:** Only for technical support and system maintenance
- **No one else:** We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties

## 5. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023)

- **Access:** Ask us what data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Fix any incorrect data via WhatsApp or through your CA
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data (subject to GST Act retention requirements)
- **Withdraw consent:** Reply "nahi" or "withdraw consent" on WhatsApp at any time

## 6. Data Security

- All data stored in India (no cross-border transfer)
- Encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access restricted to your CA only
- Audit trail on all data changes
- PII masked in all system logs

## 7. Data Retention

- Invoice images: deleted after 3 years
- Financial data: deleted after 7 years (GST audit window)
- Deletion is automatic and irreversible

## 8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact your CA or email the
Data Protection Officer at the address provided during registration.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you via WhatsApp if this policy changes materially.
Your continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
